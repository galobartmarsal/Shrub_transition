Variables Summary:

- elevation: from DME 15x15 m, ICGC https://www.icgc.cat/ca/Mapes-i-geoinformacio/Dades-i-productes/Bessons-digitals-Elevacions/Model-delevacions-del-terreny-de-15x15-m

- slope: derived from DME 15x15 m elaborated by ICGC

- average annual radiation: (10 kJ/(m2*día*micrómetro). Atlas climatico digital de la P.I. Ninyerola et al., 2005 (https://opengis.grumets.cat/wms/iberia/mms/credits_spa.htm), (https://opengis.grumets.cat/wms/iberia/mms/metadades/anual_radiacio_spa.htm). 200x200 m pixel size.

- average annual precipitation 1991-2020: (Atles climàtic de Catalunya) 1000x1000m https://sig.gencat.cat/visors/hipermapa.html

- summer precipitation: (Atles climàtic de Catalunya) 1000x1000m https://sig.gencat.cat/visors/hipermapa.html

- winter temperatura: (Atles climàtic de Catalunya) 1000x1000m https://sig.gencat.cat/visors/hipermapa.html

- average annual temperature 1991 - 2020: (Atles climàtic de Catalunya) 1000x1000m https://sig.gencat.cat/visors/hipermapa.html

- shrub distance to forests: with 1987 shrub point layer we calculated the minimum distance among one shurb point to the merge of vector forest 1987.

- TWI: (topographic wetness index): derived from 15x15 m DEM elaborated by ICGC, and function TWI (one step), from SAGA. (https://saga-gis.sourceforge.io/saga_tool_doc/9.3.2/tc_ta_hydrology_twi.html)
